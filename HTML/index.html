Meet my dog, Pongo

This is my dog, Pongo. Pongo is a Bichon Maltez.
He is so sweet and fun to play with! He is 7 years old, but he acts like a puppy. Pongo's always so full of energy and he really hates cats.
I got him for Christmas! I really like the way he yawns and the way he sighs because he sounds more like a human, than a dog.
Pongo loves to go on long walks until he gets so tired to the point where he immediately falls asleep in the grass when we get back home. Pongo's favorite sleeping position is usually belly-up, I don't know exactly why, but he seems to enjoy that very much! He moves his paws a lot when he sleeps, I think it's really cute.
Pongo's our little family member :-)
